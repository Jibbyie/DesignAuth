Join the Movement for a Sustainable Future

Welcome to Sustainable Travel, where convenience and accessibility coexist with environmentally sustainable transportation. By working together, we are creating a world where every journey is centered around sustainability. We support the worldwide purpose set by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs) by adopting more intelligent, environmentally friendly, and healthful modes of transportation.

Imagine a time when communities are healthier, the air is cleaner, and there is less traffic. Your decisions now influence the cities of tomorrow, whether you're traveling to university or just visiting the city. In order to build a more resilient, inclusive, and sustainable world, let's make every journey matter.

Why Choose Sustainable Travel?

  • Environmentally Friendly: Choose low-emission modes of transportation, such as walking, bicycling, or public transportation, to lessen your carbon footprint.
  • Cost-Effective: Students and employees can save money by avoiding costly parking fees and high petrol prices, making sustainable travel a sensible option.
  • Healthier Lifestyles: Walking or cycling can be a great way to add physical activity to your everyday commute and enhance your mental and physical health.
  • Smarter Cities: Contribute to safer, greener, and more efficient urban environments by reducing traffic congestion and air pollution.
  • Community Impact: Encourage people to join the cause by choosing sustainable options that are advantageous to all.

By choosing smarter travel, you’re not just making a personal decision—you’re playing a vital role in advancing the goals of climate action and sustainable cities.

Our Impact

Since Sustainable Travel's inception, we have assisted thousands of employees and students in adopting environmentally beneficial transportation choices. By working together, we have lowered pollution, encouraged healthy living, and improved everyone's quality of life in cities.

Here are some key achievements:

  • Over 10,000 kilometres of emissions-free commuting recorded through cycling and walking initiatives.
  • 20% reduction in single-occupancy car trips on participating campuses.
  • 500+ participants in annual Step Challenges and Cycle Challenges.

By joining us, you’re becoming part of a movement that prioritises the planet, health, and community well-being.
